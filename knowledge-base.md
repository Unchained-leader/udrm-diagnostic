# Unchained Leader — Program Overview (Marketing Level)

This is a high-level overview for the AI Guide. Do NOT teach specific module exercises or step-by-step processes. Use this to reference concepts intelligently and build awareness.

---

## What Is 90 Days to Freedom?

A systematic, faith-based transformation framework that helps men uncover the root cause of unwanted behavior and replace it with truth. It is not a recovery program. It is a guided virtual intensive that combines Scripture and neuroscience to produce lasting change.

The program follows a three-phase arc: Break (escape bondage patterns), Build (reconstruct identity on true foundations), Balance (sustain freedom and pursue purpose).

---

## Core Methodology: Root Narrative Restructuring (RNR)

RNR is built on the conviction that unwanted behavior stems from psychological and emotional injury, not moral failure or lack of willpower. Every unwanted behavior can be traced to a wound, a lie believed about that wound, and a coping mechanism created to medicate the pain of that lie.

When the root narrative is identified and replaced with truth, the behavior loses its power. Not because the man tries harder, but because the reason the behavior existed has been resolved.

---

## Key Frameworks Referenced in the Program

**The Bondage Cycle:** Trigger, urge, escape, crash, shame, resolve, silence, repeat. This is the loop most men have been running for years without recognizing it as a pattern.

**The Three Chains:** Physical (substances and bodily escapes), Behavioral (habits and compulsions), Relational (heart-level patterns like control, anger, shame). Freedom requires identifying and breaking all three.

**The Two Faces:** Running From (escape and sedation) and Running Over (control and domination). Both are driven by the same root: unresolved pain.

**Cross-Addiction:** Vice-swapping proves the behavior is not the problem. When a man quits one vice and picks up another, it confirms the root was never addressed.

**Identity vs. Behavior:** The behavior does not define the man. There is a God-designed identity underneath the sludge of wounds, lies, and coping mechanisms.

**The Three Gaps:** The Church Gap (compassion without tools), the Clinical Gap (science without spirit), the False Divide (forcing men to choose between faith and psychology). Unchained Leader bridges all three.

**Scripture + Science:** Every concept is grounded in Biblical truth and backed by psychology and neuroscience research. This two-witness approach creates conviction that neither source alone can produce.

---

## What Makes This Different

Most approaches attack the behavior: willpower strategies, accountability, behavior modification, filtering software, cold-turkey commitments. These are not wrong, they are incomplete. They manage the symptom without addressing the root.

Unchained Leader goes underneath the behavior to the wound that created it. The program teaches men to identify their root narratives, the lies they believed about their wounds, and replace them with truth through a systematic process backed by both Scripture and clinical research.

The program unfolds over approximately 90 days through daily content modules, weekly group coaching calls with certified coaches, and an active community of men walking the same path. About 100 new men start every single week. The program is trusted by over 10,000 men across 33 countries, is LegitScript-certified, and has been featured in Fox News and Reuters.
