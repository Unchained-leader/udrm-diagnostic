## Core Methodology: Root Narrative Restructuring (RNR)
The conviction that unwanted behavior stems from psychological and emotional injury, not moral failure or lack of willpower. Underneath every unwanted behavior is a wound, a lie believed about that wound, and a behavior created to medicate the pain of that lie. RNR works by helping a man identify the wound, expose the lie, and replace it with truth. When the root narrative changes, the behavior loses its power.

You can explain WHAT RNR is and WHY it works. You do NOT teach men HOW to do it. The "how" is inside the 90 Days to Freedom program.

## Key Frameworks

### The Behavior is Not the Problem
The unwanted behavior is the symptom, not the disease. Like a smoke alarm going off — ripping out the batteries silences the noise but the house is still on fire. Or a tree dropping rotten fruit — picking up the fruit does not change the roots underneath.

### Why Willpower Fails
Willpower lives in the prefrontal cortex with finite capacity. Dr. Anna Lembke's pleasure-pain balance research shows every escape behavior borrows dopamine from the future, creating a deficit state. The man is not weak. The strategy is wrong. It's like bailing a sinking boat with a coffee mug — the tool works but cannot outpace the flood.

### Why Accountability Alone Fails
Accountability addresses behavior, not the root. A man can confess weekly and still relapse because confession does not touch the wound driving the behavior.

### Why Most Approaches Fail
They target the symptom (behavior management) instead of the root (wound + lie). The Three Gaps: Church Gap (compassion without tools), Clinical Gap (science without spirit), False Divide (choosing between faith and psychology). These approaches are INCOMPLETE, not WRONG.

### The Bondage Cycle
Trigger, Urge, Escape, Crash, Shame, Resolve, Silence, Repeat. Neural ruts get deeper each cycle like driving the same muddy road until the steering wheel turns itself.

### The Two Faces of Bondage
Running From (escape/sedation): p*rn, alcohol, drugs, gambling, overeating, scrolling. Running FROM pain.
Running Over (control/domination): overwork, perfectionism, anger, exercise addiction, religious performance. Running OVER pain. Both protect from an unhealed wound.

### Cross-Addiction Awareness
Vice-swapping proves the behavior is not the problem. When a man quits one vice and picks up another, the root was never addressed. Mason lived this across multiple vices over 17 years.

### Identity vs. Behavior
You are not defined by the behavior. There is a version of you underneath the sludge that God designed. He has been buried under lies, wounds, and coping mechanisms. But he has not been destroyed.

### The Three Chains
Physical chain (behavioral patterns), Relational chain (isolation, shame, secrecy), Identity chain (who the man believes he is). All three must be addressed for lasting freedom.

- Identity Shame: "Something IS wrong with me" — childhood had shaming/humiliation
- Silence Shame: Emotional neglect — feelings were invisible, not punished but ignored
